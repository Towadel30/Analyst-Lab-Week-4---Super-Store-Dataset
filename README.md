# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard

## 📖 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of **Week 4** of the **AnalystLab Africa Data Analytics Internship Program**.

The objective was to transform raw retail sales data into meaningful business insights using **Power BI**. An interactive dashboard was developed to help stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), analyze sales trends, evaluate product performance, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses require a clear understanding of their sales performance to maximize profitability and improve operational efficiency.

This dashboard was created to answer key business questions, including:

- Which product category generates the highest sales?
- Which customer segment contributes the most revenue?
- How do sales change over time?
- Which products perform best?
- Which regions generate the highest sales?

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Dataset:** Superstore Sales Dataset

The dataset contains retail transaction records, including:

- Order Date
- Sales
- Profit
- Customer Segment
- Product Category
- Region
- Country
- Product Name

| KPI | Value |
|------|-------|
| Total Sales | $2M |
| Total Profit | $286K |
| Profit Margin | 12.47% |
| Total Orders | 5,009 |

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- The business generated approximately **$2 million** in total sales.
- Total profit reached approximately **$286K**, resulting in a **12.47%** profit margin.
- Technology recorded the highest sales among all product categories.
- Consumer customers contributed the largest share of overall sales.
- Sales increased steadily toward the end of the year, peaking in **November**.
- The Canon imageCLASS product generated the highest sales among all products.
- Interactive filters allow users to analyze performance by category, region, segment, and year.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Increase investment in the Technology category to sustain revenue growth.
- Strengthen customer retention strategies for the Consumer segment.
- Improve marketing efforts for lower-performing product categories.
- Prepare inventory ahead of peak sales periods during the final quarter of the year.
- Develop targeted strategies to improve performance in lower-performing regions.
